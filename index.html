About Us

WE ARE EDaur

FREE EDUCATION

We believe that good education is a foundation for a better future. That is why we provide knowledge without gaining any monetary benefit from students.

STUDENT ORIENTED

Our prime focus is students, and we are dedicated to creating a one-stop resource for the leaders of tomorrow.

WONDERFUL READING EXPERIENCE

We ensure our magazine has a premium feel and once you start reading it, you embark upon an unforgettable journey.

SOURCE OF ENTERTAINMENT

Chilling on a couch or traveling on a bus? Our magazines are filled with brain teasers and interesting stuff to read making it a perfect source of entertainment.

RICH AND CURATED CONTENT

Each and every word included in the magazine is carefully curated by our excellent editorial team. It is a huge plethora of knowledge that would definitely help your mind grow.
